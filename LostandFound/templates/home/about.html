About This Service

Amherst Lost & Found is a platform designed to help the Amherst College community reconnect with their lost belongings. Whether you've lost something or found an item, this service is here to assist you.

Our mission is to make it easier for students, faculty, and staff to report and recover lost items, fostering a sense of trust and community on campus.
